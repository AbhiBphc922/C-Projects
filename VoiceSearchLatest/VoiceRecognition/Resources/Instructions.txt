
Voice PM Search form:

Say the name clearly to populate in the text box.

1. 'exit'- To Exit the application(Shortcut-Alt+F4,File->Exit).

2. 'type'- Displays a text box for us to enter a name.

Other functions-

1. Help- Displays this window.(Shortcut-Alt+H,File->Help)

2. Start- Enables the microphone for voice input.(Shortcut-Alt+T)

3. Stop- To stop microphone.(Shortcut-Alt+S)

Enter Name form:

Spell out the name and it populates in the text box.

1. Save- Used to add the name in the text box to the database.(Shortcut-Ctrl+S)

2. Clear- Used to clear the name in the text box.(Shortcut-Ctrl+C)

3. Back- Used to revert to the PM Search form.(Shortcut-Esc)
